Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This site is a free, interactive tool. We collect as little data as possible and will never sell it. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to subscribe via the form at the bottom of the page. We use this to send occasional updates. You can unsubscribe any time from any email we send.
  • Anonymous usage data — we use Google Analytics to understand how people find and use the site (pages visited, time on site, general location by country/region). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

What we don't collect

  • No user accounts or passwords
  • No payment information
  • No precise location data
  • Nothing beyond what's listed above

Browser localStorage

This site saves your last-visited tab (Energy, Water, or Sources) in your browser's localStorage so you land back where you left off. This data never leaves your device and contains no personal information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure site traffic. Google may place cookies to track sessions. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

ConvertKit (email)

If you subscribe to our email list, your email address is stored and managed by Kit (ConvertKit). Their privacy policy is available at kit.com/privacy. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone, ever.
